## 4. Specific Feature Brainstorming: The "Kill Shots"

These are four hyper-specific agentic workflows, built on our core ADRs, that are impossible with traditional technology and would provide Theoria with an unassailable competitive advantage.

1.  **The "Post-Acute Guardian" Agent:** This agent is the embodiment of proactive, value-based care. It monitors a recently discharged CHF patient living in one of your communities.
    *   **(ADR-007):** It ingests real-time data from the patient’s Withings smart scale and Oura Ring.
    *   **(ADR-006):** On Tuesday morning, it detects a 3-pound weight gain over 48 hours and a 15% drop in sleep quality. This triggers a context rehydration event.
    *   **(ADR-001):** The agent immediately pulls the patient's full FHIR record, noting they are on a diuretic (Furosemide).
    *   **(ADR-003 & ADR-008):** The LangGraph agent, with a 98% confidence score, concludes this is a high-risk indicator for CHF exacerbation. It uses A2A communication to send a P2 alert directly to the on-site nurse's workflow, including the data trend, the patient's record, and a recommended action: "Administer 40mg Furosemide IV and re-check weight in 4 hours."
    *   **Impact:** A potential rehospitalization is averted for the cost of a single API call, directly protecting your shared savings in the ACO REACH model.

2.  **The "Zero-Touch Revenue" Agent (Chronic Care Management):** This agent ensures you capture all deserved revenue under CCM and RPM CPT codes, which are notoriously difficult to track.
    *   **(ADR-001 & ADR-006):** The agent maintains a persistent, rehydrated context of all non-face-to-face interactions for eligible patients—secure messages, phone calls, clinician notes, and time spent reviewing wearable data.
    *   **(ADR-007):** It automatically logs the time spent by clinicians reviewing the wearable data feeds as eligible RPM time.
    *   **(ADR-003):** On the 25th day of the month, the agent's state machine checks if a patient has crossed the 20-minute threshold for CPT 99490 (CCM).
    *   **(ADR-008):** Once the threshold is met, it uses an A2A message to instruct the "Revenue Cycle Agent" to add the CPT code and a supporting summary note to the patient's next claim.
    *   **Impact:** Captures an additional $60-$150 per eligible patient per month in high-margin revenue that is often left on the table due to manual tracking failures.

3.  **The "ACO REACH Quality Score" Agent:** This agent works tirelessly in the background to maximize your quality score, directly impacting your financial performance in VBC contracts.
    *   **(ADR-001):** The agent continuously scans the entire patient population in your FHIR database.
    *   **(ADR-003):** It identifies patients with open "care gaps" as defined by ACO REACH quality measures (e.g., a diabetic patient overdue for an HbA1c test, a patient with hypertension without a recent BP reading).
    *   **(ADR-008):** It uses A2A to communicate with the "Patient Communication Agent," which initiates an automated, personalized SMS/voice call to the patient to schedule the necessary appointment or home visit.
    *   **Impact:** Moves VBC quality management from a retrospective reporting exercise to a proactive, automated process, ensuring you meet performance benchmarks and maximize shared savings.

4.  **The "PE-Readiness" Agent:** This agent provides the on-demand, board-level insights required by Amulet Capital.
    *   **(ADR-003):** A designated executive at Theoria or Amulet can prompt this LangGraph agent via a secure interface: "Show me the 30-day rehospitalization rate for post-op orthopedic patients in our Michigan facilities compared to our Ohio facilities, and highlight the top 3 contributing factors for any variance."
    *   **(ADR-001):** The agent queries the entire FHIR data lake, pulling encounter data, diagnoses, and facility information.
    *   **(ADR-006):** It ensures the data is fresh as of the last few seconds.
    *   **Result:** Within a minute, it generates a concise report with data visualizations, identifying that the Ohio facilities have a 4% higher rate, likely due to lower adherence to post-op physical therapy, which it cross-referenced from clinician notes.
    *   **Impact:** Turns weeks of data analysis work into a 60-second query, enabling true data-driven governance and strategic decision-making.

## 5. Implementation Roadmap for Theoria Medical

Our approach is designed for zero disruption to your current 24/7 operations, demonstrating value at every step before broader adoption. We propose a 90-day pilot within a single, representative state (e.g., Michigan).

**Phase 1: Foundation & Passive Analysis (Days 1-30)**
*   **Action:** We establish a secure, HIPAA-compliant connection to one of your facility's data sources (EHR or data warehouse). MedOS begins ingesting data into a dedicated, isolated tenant (`ADR-002`) in a read-only capacity.
*   **Goal:** Demonstrate our data ingestion and normalization capabilities. Our AI agents will "shadow" your current operations.
*   **Deliverable:** A "VBC Opportunity Report" identifying missed revenue opportunities (e.g., CCM codes) and care gaps that our agents detected from the raw data.
*   **Impact:** Zero operational risk. Immediate insight into the potential financial and clinical upside.

**Phase 2: Proactive Monitoring Pilot (Days 31-60)**
*   **Action:** We deploy the "Post-Acute Guardian" agent for a cohort of 50-100 high-risk patients. We will work with you to provide these patients with a wearable device (e.g., Oura Ring).
*   **Goal:** Prove that our proactive monitoring can reduce clinical events and operational load.
*   **Deliverable:** All agent-generated alerts are routed to a designated Theoria clinical lead for review and validation. We will produce a weekly report comparing alerts, interventions, and outcomes for the pilot cohort against a control group.
*   **Impact:** Tangible, data-backed evidence of reduced rehospitalization risk and improved clinical efficiency.

**Phase 3: Live Workflow Integration (Days 61-90)**
*   **Action:** For the pilot facility, we turn on the "Zero-Touch Revenue" and "ACO REACH Quality Score" agents. The output will be queued in a review dashboard for your billing and quality teams to approve before any claim is submitted or patient is contacted.
*   **Goal:** Demonstrate end-to-end workflow automation and the accuracy of our AI-driven administrative functions.
*   **Deliverable:** A live dashboard showing captured revenue, closed care gaps, and time saved by your staff. We will provide a comprehensive ROI analysis based on the 90-day pilot.
*   **Impact:** A clear, undeniable business case for a state-wide and subsequent national rollout, fully de-risked and validated within your own operational environment.
