## 🚀 Key Strategic Pivots for MedOS

Given Dr. Di Rezze's background, pitching MedOS as just an OS for a "10-person clinic" leaves massive value on the table. We need to frame MedOS as the **ultimate engine for scaled, value-based care in post-acute and long-term care (LTC) settings.**

### 1. Shift from "Clinic OS" to "Distributed Care OS"
- **The Gap:** SNFs and Assisted Living facilities have notoriously fragmented communication. Nurses use one system, remote doctors (like Theoria's) use another.
- **The Agentic Solution:** MedOS should act as the intelligent bridge. Agents that ingest data from SNF EHRs (PointClickCare, MatrixCare) and translate it instantly into actionable FHIR resources for the remote physician's workspace.

### 2. Hyper-Focus on Chronic Care Management (CCM) & Remote Patient Monitoring (RPM)
- **The Gap:** Billing for CCM (CPT 99490) requires exact tracking of 20+ minutes of non-face-to-face clinical staff time per month. It is notoriously difficult to track manually.
- **The Agentic Solution:** An "Auditor Agent" that silently tracks all background work (chart review, care coordination calls, lab reviews) per patient, aggregates the time, and auto-generates the billing claim the moment the 20-minute threshold is crossed. This is pure, uncaptured revenue for a company like Theoria.

### 3. The 24/7 "Handoff" Agent (Critical for Telemedicine)
- **The Gap:** Telemedicine across multiple facilities requires flawless shift handoffs. Missing a critical lab result during a shift change can be fatal.
- **The Agentic Solution:** "Shift Summary Agents." When Dr. A logs off and Dr. B logs on, the agent generates an instant, priority-ranked briefing of all high-acuity patients across all 50+ facilities they are covering, highlighting pending labs or degrading vitals.

### 4. Predictive Readmission Agents (Value-Based Care)
- **The Gap:** Value-based care models penalize hospital readmissions.
- **The Agentic Solution:** Agents continuously scanning incoming RPM data (weight scales, BP cuffs) and nurse notes to predict readmission risk *before* the patient decompensates. If a heart failure patient gains 3 lbs in 24 hours, the agent automatically drafts a diuretic order and prompts the covering doctor to approve it.

## 💡 Missing Features in the Current Masterplan

1. **Facility-Level Dashboards:** The current plan focuses on Provider/Patient/Payer. We need a **Facility Console** (for the Director of Nursing at the SNF to see all patients managed by the MedOS-powered practice).
2. **Device Integration Layer:** A dedicated module for RPM devices (cellular blood pressure cuffs, continuous glucose monitors).
3. **Automated Credentialing Agent:** For a fast-growing company, onboarding new doctors to different state boards and hospital systems is a nightmare. An agent that auto-fills and manages CAQH and state licensing applications.
4. **Telemedicine Native Recording:** Whisper-native transcription directly from WebRTC video feeds, not just in-person ambient listening.
